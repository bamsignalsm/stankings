# Stankings Legacy Live

**Brand name:** Stankings Legacy Live  
**Owned by:** Stankings Legacy Ltd  
**Entity type:** Umbrella brand — **not** a subsidiary, legal entity, or standalone company  
**Category:** Corporate Live Experiences  
**Business sector (classification only):** Community & Live Experiences  
**Registry (SSOT):** `LEGACY_LIVE_REGISTRY` → `src/lib/legacy-live/registry.ts`

---

## Constitutional objective

> To educate, connect, inspire and celebrate individuals, families, professionals, entrepreneurs and communities through transformational live experiences that advance the constitutional mission and legacy of Stankings Legacy Ltd.

## Vision

One recognizable live-experiences brand for every Stankings Legacy physical programme — awards, conferences, summits, signature experiences, and community education — without turning operating companies into events businesses.

## Mission

Own and operate flagship live programmes that strengthen relationships, leadership, corporate authority, and community impact across the Stankings ecosystem.

## Strategic purpose

Advance the constitutional mission of Stankings Legacy Ltd by creating recurring, institutional live experiences — not one-off campaigns — that educate, connect, inspire and celebrate.

## Core values

- Constitutional purpose before spectacle
- Programme ownership stays with Stankings Legacy Ltd
- Hosts do not become events companies
- Independent programme identity under one umbrella
- Generational continuity — editions and archives, not disposable events
- Trust, dignity and excellence in every live experience

## Canonical hierarchy

```
Stankings Legacy Ltd
└── Stankings Legacy Live (Umbrella Brand)
    └── Programme / Series
          └── Annual Edition          e.g. Singles Synergy 2027
                └── Event             e.g. Day 1
                      └── Session     e.g. Opening Ceremony, Leadership Session
```

**Awards (institutional model):**

```
Award Institution
└── Annual Edition
      ├── Award Categories
      ├── Nominations / Nominees
      ├── Jury & Judging Criteria
      ├── Winners Archive / Hall of Fame
      ├── Annual Reports
      └── Award Ceremony (event + sessions)
```

This hierarchy exists now so tickets, schedules, and archives can attach later without redesign.

## Ownership chain

```
Stankings Legacy Ltd
        owns
        ↓
Stankings Legacy Live
        manages
        ↓
Programmes → Editions → Events → Sessions
```

## Operating model

1. **Owner:** Stankings Legacy Ltd
2. **Umbrella:** Stankings Legacy Live
3. **Host:** Subsidiary provides hosting identity (BamSignal, Times, Institute, …)
4. **Venue:** Stankings Hotel & Suites (external until operational)
5. **Media:** Stankings Times Ltd
6. **Payments (future):** BayRight Ltd
7. **Identity (future):** Stankings Passport
8. **Marketplace (future):** Yike Ltd
