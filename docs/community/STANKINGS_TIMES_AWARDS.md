## Institutional model

```
Award Institution          ← Stankings Times Awards
└── Annual Edition         ← e.g. Stankings Times Awards 2027
      ├── Award Categories
      ├── Nominations / Nominees
      ├── Jury
      ├── Judging Criteria
      ├── Winners Archive
      ├── Hall of Fame
      ├── Annual Reports
      └── Award Ceremony   ← event day(s) + sessions
```

This allows nominations, jury, categories, archives, and hall of fame later **without redesign**.
