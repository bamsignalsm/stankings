# Signature Experiences

**Kind:** Reserved architecture under [Stankings Legacy Live](./STANKINGS_LEGACY_LIVE.md)  
**Owned by:** Stankings Legacy Ltd  
**Registry id:** `signature-experiences`  
**Status:** Reserved  
**Replaces:** “Future Conferences” (broader label)

---

## Purpose

Reserve space for additional live experiences without structural redesign:

- Conferences
- Exhibitions
- Expos
- Retreats
- Gala dinners
- Trade fairs
- Investment forums

## Operating company — assigned per purpose

Signature Experiences are **not** pre-assigned to one subsidiary. Stankings Legacy Ltd owns them; the operating company is chosen by purpose for each edition:

| Purpose | Operating company |
|---------|-------------------|
| Technology | The Stankings Institute |
| Real Estate | Stanhan Real Estate Ltd |
| Media | Stankings Times Ltd |
| Hospitality | Stankings Hotel & Suites Ltd |
| Manufacturing | Shodis Industries Ltd |
| Finance | BayRight Ltd |
| Community | BamSignal Ltd |
| Education / Leadership | The Stankings Institute |

Source: `SIGNATURE_EXPERIENCE_OPERATOR_MAP` in `src/lib/legacy-live/registry.ts`.

## Hierarchy

```
Signature Experiences (reserve)
└── Named experience (series)
      └── Edition
            └── Event
                  └── Session
```

## Rules

- Remains under Stankings Legacy Live
- Does not create a new company
- Does not force BamSignal (or any host) to become an events platform
- Media default: Stankings Times Ltd
- Venue default: Stankings Hotel & Suites Ltd (external until operational)
