## Business division

Media, Publishing & Corporate Authority

## Mission

Serve as the official media and communications arm of Stankings Legacy Ltd. Build corporate authority, strengthen public trust, promote every ecosystem company through owned media, and create long-term relationships with business leaders across Africa.

## Core responsibilities

- Digital publishing
- Daily business news
- Executive interviews
- Podcasts
- Industry reports
- Investigative journalism
- Corporate storytelling
- Thought leadership

## Flagship products

- [Stankings Times Awards](../community/STANKINGS_TIMES_AWARDS.md) — **award institution** under [Stankings Legacy Live](../community/STANKINGS_LEGACY_LIVE.md) (not merely a ceremony)
- Executive Briefing
- Founder Interviews
- Leadership Series

## Stankings Legacy Live

Stankings Times Ltd **hosts** the Stankings Times Awards institution. Ownership remains with **Stankings Legacy Ltd**. Architecture:

```
Award Institution → Annual Edition → Ceremony
(+ categories, nominations, jury, winners archive, hall of fame, annual reports)
```

Times also provides media coverage for all Legacy Live programmes.

## Ecosystem relationships

**Provides:** media, public relations, corporate communications, awards, brand visibility, executive influence.

**Supports:** every Stankings subsidiary through strategic communication; all [Stankings Legacy Live](../community/STANKINGS_LEGACY_LIVE.md) programmes with coverage and publicity.

**Depends on:** BamSignal (identity/community), Stankings Hotel & Suites (awards and summit venues), BayRight (payments for programmes).
