## Out of Phase 1 careers org

Institute, Logistics, Hotel & Suites, Shodis remain on the public companies site; no launch vacancies unless added later. GENERAL engineering roles are posted under `hq`.

## Per-company capabilities

Each company supports: Departments, Roles, Managers, Employees, Company Settings (in Energy), Company Dashboard (Office + Energy stats), Company Analytics (Energy).
